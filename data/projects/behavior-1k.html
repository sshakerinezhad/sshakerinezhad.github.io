Meet Arthur. For six weeks, he refused to do his chores. He'd just stand there trembling because he realized the best way to minimize error was to never move an inch.

We eventually fixed him by blinding him (60% proprioceptive dropout) and forcing him to commit to his decisions (receding horizon control). In the final 48 hours, he panicked his way from a 0% to a 1.78% score. It sounds modest, but watching him finally pick up that trash felt like watching a child walk.
